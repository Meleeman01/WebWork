FML-FTW

FML-FTW is a webcomic that studies relationships, and how they pertain to ones happiness. Tim, A fox is wanting a significant other that will make life more bearable to live in a world with bullies, gossip girls, and a complacent, judgmental society. But will relationship offer true peace of mind to him?

Small update

January 29, 2016

So College is kicking my ass. lol. I have project management which is useful but boring, and english comp 2 which I don't like writing papers. (odd cause I'm a writer and story teller and I hate writing.). I suppose it's not the writing itself that I hate, it's the subjects, and if i'm not into a subject, or totally in love with it, I won't write it. hence why I spent 5 years on this comic writing and refining it to what it is now. I do try hard to make quality content, and I hold myself up to a timely standard when I do have time.

So have i been doing anything for the comic? well no not really. just general self promotion and such, because its much easier to tell people about it, especially when i'm kind of stuck with college work. i could resolve to work harder maybe. but I love myself too much to do that. I love sleep. and i love doing work when i'm fully rested, and have a coffee to get me going. it's not too much to ask right? well. i figure If I don't want to spend the next 5 years comicking and not have even a book out I'm probably going much too slow. i've yet to do a double update, but since my colorist is well idunno... not in the mood to help I believe, I'm kind of stuck with just me doing the coloring. I'll leave her screen name on it for keepsakes. she'll probably want some royalties im sure. I feel bad about not paying her though. if I can at least get enough traffic in ads i could at least pay her 40 dollars per page. or 10 bucks per hour.

Anyways, I would like to thank all my supporters of my comic for bearing with me, and I will keep my promises just as I have before. the year prep work seems to be working for me, and i'm glad. Until next time friends!

Oh. and if you would like to comment on the comments thing, the passcode for the comments is the main male protagonist in the story.
